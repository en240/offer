Financial Terms
The length of a car loan will dictate how long it will take to pay your financial contract. In most cases, a lender will offer you a loan with a term of 36 to 72 months, in that period you will need to repay the price of your vehicle plus interest. Interest rates and terms offered may differ and depend on multiple factors, like your credit score and down payment.
It is crucial to thoroughly acknowledge that the term of your financial contract also has an important role in calculating your interest rate and consequently, your monthly payment. The APR range is 3.49 +% and the average interest rates are as follows:
36 Months: 3.68%
48 Months: 3.79%
60 Months: 3.95%
72 Months: 3.81%
